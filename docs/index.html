About me

Hello, my name is Paola García. I am a student in the first semester of the Visual Media Production Certificate at the City College of San Francisco. I worked previously in Project Management, defining processes and implementing new technologies, which has been very interesting. However, now I am interested in learning new skills. Graphic design would allow me to participate in creative projects, focused on developing products that effectively communicate their message. This portfolio includes the final works created in the Adobe suite during the VMD 105 Digital Skill class of the fall 2021 semester.
