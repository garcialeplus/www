Working with Illustrator

Illustrator was the second application used in the class. We learned to use vectors. The final work was to make our logo. I decided to make the logo with my initials to use as an image on my photography page. I really liked being able to combine the image of my initials on top of one of my photos and use text in the same project. Illustrator blows my mind and I imagine endless things that I can create. I can see myself participating in projects where I can design the image of a company or person. Personally, I used this application to design the invitations for my wedding and I loved it.
